Alert Thresholds
🟢 Level 0 — Safe
  • Temp < 38°C
  • Flame < 15%
  • Gas < 25%
  • Green LED ON, silent
🟡 Level 1 — Caution
  • Temp 38–45°C, OR
  • Flame 15–35%, OR
  • Gas 25–40%
  • Yellow LED ON, silent
🔴 Level 2 — Warning
  • Temp 45–55°C, OR
  • Flame 35–60%, OR
  • Gas 40–60%
  • Red LED ON + 3 beeps
🚨 Level 3 — Fire!
  • Temp ≥ 55°C, OR
  • Flame ≥ 60%, OR
  • Gas ≥ 60%
  • All LEDs + rapid alarm
